H1
First level of heading ( BIGTEXT ).
<h1> BOOM! </h1>
H2
Second level of heading.
<h2> boom... </h2>
H3
Third level of heading (small text).
<h3> not so boom. </h3>

<ol>
<li>Viva la vida</li>
</ol>

<ul>
<li>Comic Sans!</li>
</ul>

List item.
<li>Comic Sans... not good.</li>
